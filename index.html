      How to Use This Calendar

      1. Tap a date in the calendar above.
      2. Scheduled calls for that day will appear below.
      3. Tap the "Schedule a Call" button or the date again to open the scheduling form.
      4. To delete a call, tap the 🗑️ icon next to it.
      5. Please don't call before 10:30am or after 8:30pm :)

      The goal is to help Grandma stay connected and mentally active by hearing from everyone who loves her.
